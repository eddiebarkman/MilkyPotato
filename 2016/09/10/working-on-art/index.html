Working on Art

Kinao September 10, 2016 No comments

Okay so we’ve got our story more or less completed and have been working on artwork! By artwork I mean character drawings for the game. Miru has been busy working hard on the character’s faces and outfits.

Meanwhile I’ve been learning how to create pixel art. We’ve definitely decided that we want our game to have a retro JRPG feel to it. Influenced by the Tales series and other games!

I’m learning that there is a lot of work that goes into sprites. Especially if you want them to move smoothly. We are testing out different styles for now.

Here’s a random version of what I’m working on… Assuming the gif works. Definitely not final art, I’m still learning!
