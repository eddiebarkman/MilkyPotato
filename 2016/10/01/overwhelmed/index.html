Overwhelmed!

Kinao October 1, 2016 No comments

Holy wow there is a lot to do! However it’s so much fun seeing stuff start to slowly come together. We have a good amount of artwork done for the characters, thanks to Miru! She is still working hard on the many different characters we have. Meanwhile I’m still working on sprites. I made a few full sprite sheets for characters however we are thinking of going in a different direction, a possibly more simplistic style. So I might be starting from scratch…Which is fine, more practice! This is one of our potatoes. Isn’t he cute? 

 

I’m also working on some music! Which is a lot of fun! Maybe I’ll post a link to one of the songs on this post once I figure out a good place to upload it.

I started playing the violin when I was in 4th Grade. I wasn’t very good but I had fun. I also started learning piano and guitar for a few years in college. I’ve written a few simple songs in the past on piano. Nothing extremely complicated or detailed. Just things I found pleasant to hear and play. So that’s what I’m trying to bring to whatever music I end up creating for our game.

So much to do!!!

 

-Kinao
